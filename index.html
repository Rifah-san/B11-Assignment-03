FAQs

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by browsing our categories, adding items to your cart, and proceeding to checkout. Simply enter your delivery details, choose a payment method, and confirm your order.

What are the delivery options and timings?

We offer same-day and scheduled delivery options. Orders placed before 12:00 pm are delivered on the same day, while others are scheduled for the next available slot

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile banking, and cash on delivery (COD). You can select your preferred method during checkout.
